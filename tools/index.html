Tools

We are building tools that embody augmented intelligence principles — keeping humans in control while amplifying capability.

Our Philosophy

Every tool we build follows a simple principle: the human directs, the machine assists. No black boxes. No tools that make decisions for you. Tools that make your decisions better.

We are in the early stages of building our tool suite. The first tool — Metron — is a local AI model management system designed for people who want to run AI on their own hardware, with full control over their data and models.

Metron

Local AI model management. Load, swap, and configure models on your own machine. No cloud dependency. No data leaves your computer.

Status: In development
