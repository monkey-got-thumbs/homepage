The Skills Gap Is Real

AI-related course enrolments are up 160% year-on-year. 75% of knowledge workers are already using AI at work. But most are using it badly — accepting output uncritically, missing hallucinations, building dependencies instead of capabilities.

The people who will thrive are not the ones who use AI the most. They are the ones who use it with the most skill: directing it effectively, verifying its output, and applying human judgment where it matters.
