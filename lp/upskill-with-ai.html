From using AI to using it well

Lots of people are using AI now, and it's already part of most working days. The difference that matters isn't how much you use it — it's how well: checking its output, spotting when it's confidently wrong, and keeping your own judgment in the loop.

And that's the good part: using AI well is a skill anyone can pick up. No coding, no maths — just a few good habits, which is exactly what these paths teach.
