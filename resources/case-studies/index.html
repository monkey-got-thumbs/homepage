Case Study 1: The Power of Saying No

A technology consultant pushed back against a startup CEO's demand to use TypeScript, reframing the conversation around the real goal: reaching product-market fit before running out of cash.

Results:

  • Launched in 7 weeks (vs. 10-11 with TypeScript)
  • First paying customer after 2 weeks
  • Strategic pivot at 4 months saved 6 months of development
  • $500K funding round secured on real traction

This case demonstrates that augmented intelligence is not just about AI tools — it is about the human judgment, context awareness, and professional courage that no machine can provide. Read the full analysis.
