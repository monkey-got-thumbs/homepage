Resources

A curated collection of books, research, tools, and external resources for learning augmented intelligence and human-AI collaboration.

Essential Reading

  • The Beginning of Infinity by David Deutsch — the intellectual foundation for "hard-to-vary explanations" that underpin our framework.
  • Human + Machine by Paul Daugherty & H. James Wilson — Accenture's framework for human-AI collaboration in business.
  • Co-Intelligence by Ethan Mollick — practical strategies for working alongside AI in knowledge work.
  • Thinking, Fast and Slow by Daniel Kahneman — the psychology behind bounded rationality and cognitive biases that affect AI interaction.
  • Drive by Daniel Pink — the science of purpose, mastery, and autonomy that shapes agency and motivation.

Research & Reports

  • Stanford HAI AI Index Report — annual comprehensive assessment of the state of AI across industry, research, and policy.
  • WEF Future of Jobs Report — the leading analysis of AI's impact on global employment and skills requirements.
  • Harvard/BCG Generative AI Study — the definitive study on how AI-assisted workers produce 40% higher quality work when directed effectively.
  • Microsoft Work Trend Index — longitudinal data on how knowledge workers are actually adopting AI tools.
