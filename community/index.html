how we treat each other

The spirit of the place

A few gentle promises about what it feels like to be here.

Curious, not scared

AI is new and a bit strange — that's allowed. We meet it with curiosity instead of panic, and we never make you feel daft for asking.

Honest, not hyped

No breathless "AI will change your life" talk. Just real, plain experiences — what worked, what didn't, and what's worth your time.

You stay in charge

AI is a helper, not the boss. We're here to make you more confident and more in control — never to replace your own good judgment.

Kindness compounds

What you learn is worth more when you pass it on. A small tip shared today helps someone feel braver tomorrow. That's the whole idea.
