Notes

A growing web of short, linked ideas about augmented intelligence — built around a claim worth testing: AI is one tool for thinking, not the thinker. Each note states one claim and links to the others. Follow a thread; you won't lose your place.

How to read this: these are evergreen notes — each titled with a single claim, kept short, and linked to its neighbours. Start anywhere and follow the links. "Linked from" at the foot of each note shows what points back to it.
