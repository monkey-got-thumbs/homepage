Our Philosophy

Every tool we build follows the same principles that underpin augmented intelligence:

  • You stay in control. AI assists. You decide what to trust, what to verify, and what to override.
  • Your data stays yours. Local-first by default. Cloud is opt-in, not mandatory.
  • No vendor lock-in. Open formats, standard protocols, portable data.
  • Built to last. Rust where performance matters. Simple architecture over clever abstraction.
