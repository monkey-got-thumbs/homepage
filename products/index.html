A couple of little helpers

We don't have a big catalogue — just two small things we built to make AI feel friendlier and more useful. Here's what each one actually does for you, in plain words.

chatyman

A friendly AI helper you can pop onto your own website.

Got a website? chatyman is a little chat bubble in the corner that answers your visitors' questions — kindly, and in your words. It reads your own pages, so it talks about your place, not the whole internet.

  • It only answers from what you've told it, so it won't make things up.
  • The chats happen right there in your visitor's browser — nothing's sent off to be stored elsewhere.
  • Free to run on your own site — or let us host it for you.

Agent Builder Advisor

A free helper that makes the way you ask AI much better.

When you ask AI to do a job for you, the way you word it changes everything. Paste in what you've written, add a couple of examples, and the Advisor shows you how it works (preview): one round of friendly, honest feedback — what's working, what might trip you up, and exactly how to make it clearer.

  • You get a quick, expert read — not a chat that goes round in circles.
  • It shows you what your wording would actually produce, so there are no surprises.
  • Free to use, no sign-up.

Why does it help to tell AI exactly what you want?

AI can't read your mind — it fills the gaps with a safe, generic guess. The more you say about the tone, the length, and the outcome you're after, the closer it gets to what you actually meant.
how we build

A few quiet promises

Whatever we make, we hold to the same simple ideas — the same ones we teach about.

  • You stay in charge. AI helps; you decide what to keep, what to change, and what to ignore. It never takes the wheel.
  • Your information stays yours. We keep things on your own device wherever we can, and we'd never quietly hand your details around.
  • Nothing locks you in. If you ever want to walk away, you can — your stuff comes with you.
  • Made to be reliable. We'd rather make something simple and steady than something clever and fragile.
