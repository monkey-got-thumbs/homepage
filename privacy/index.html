Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What We Collect

We collect minimal data, only what is needed to operate the site and improve your experience:

  • Email address — if you subscribe to our newsletter. Used only to send you content. Never sold or shared with third parties.
  • Analytics data — anonymous page views, scroll depth, and click patterns via Google Analytics 4 (when configured). IP addresses are anonymised.
  • UTM parameters — campaign tracking data from URL parameters, stored in your browser's session storage.

How We Use It

  • Newsletter emails: to deliver augmented intelligence content you have requested.
  • Analytics: to understand which content is useful and improve the site.
  • Campaign data: to understand which channels drive engagement.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell your data to third parties.
  • We do not share your email with anyone.
  • We do not use your data for advertising beyond our own campaigns.
  • We do not track you across other websites.

Your Rights

You can unsubscribe from the newsletter at any time. To request deletion of your data, email hello@monkey-got-thumbs.com and we will remove your information within 5 business days.

Cookies

This site uses minimal cookies for analytics purposes only. No advertising cookies. No third-party tracking cookies.
